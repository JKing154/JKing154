Trip Cock Impact Test Bench

Designed and built an impact test bench from concept to physical completion to validate trip cocks and future products requiring a 60 mph strike. The bench needed to be faster than the existing setup, so I was tasked with redesigning the lever arm entirely.

Using dynamics calculations to determine the required geometry and Ansys FEA to verify the arm could survive its own impact loads, I cut the lever arm weight in half — from 40 lbs to 19.8 lbs — while hitting the 60 mph actuation target. The redesign also improves repeatability, making the bench usable as a standardized platform for future validation programs beyond the trip cock.

Hose Flex Test Rack

Responsible for sourcing, redesigning components, and producing engineering drawings for a hose flex test rack that needs to be replicated across two additional units. The racks are deployed to international test locations to ensure consistent methodology across the company's global sites — a $20K+ project per unit in parts alone.

Managing procurement directly with suppliers, resolving lead time issues, and maintaining the schedule across a multi-site deployment.

Test Floor Experience

Working daily in a large-scale testing environment: setting up and operating vibration tables, Thermotron thermal chambers (hot/cold cycling), and pneumatic test fixtures under constant cycling. Responsible for configuring test setups, maintaining instrumentation, and ensuring test conditions match specifications before any validation run begins.

What They Do

Skip-Line builds measurement devices for state DOTs: tracking paint line thickness, paint volume, and glass bead distribution on highway markings, all feeding into a cloud platform for fleet reporting. I came back for a second summer, which gave me a lot more ownership over real projects.

Laser Fixtures & Enclosure Design

The old laser cutter fixtures were a pain to swap out. Getting a new enclosure lined up meant unbolting things, realigning by hand, and hoping it was consistent. I redesigned them so enclosures just drop in and cut accurately every time, no fuss. That change alone cut machine downtime by 25%. I also helped develop a modular enclosure platform where one base housing covers multiple product variants, just swapping a side panel. Fewer unique parts, simpler inventory, and it helped reduce tariff exposure too.

NRE Pricing & AI Research

I looked at how the company was pricing NRE requests from customers and found the rates were too low for what was actually being asked of engineers. I recommended adjusting them to protect engineering time without changing what customers paid overall. That recommendation got implemented. I also put together a research presentation on AI tools for the executive team and CEO, which ended up leading to a company-wide rollout of tools like Gamma.
